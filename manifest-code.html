Manifest Library

How to use

The Manifest language is a secret sign language designed to be both extremely fast to use, and subtle.

When "talking", you:

  • Don't care about your hands' or arms' positions.
  • Use your left hand to identify the kind of word(person, verb, adjective, place, etc.)
  • Use your right hand to identify the actual word.
  • May make multiple signs to make 1 word. (roots, suffix, affix, etc.)
